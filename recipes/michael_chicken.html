Michael's Chicken

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup cider vinegar
  • ¼ cup chili sauce
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 8 chicken thighs

Directions

  • Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Step 2 In a medium saucepan over medium heat, blend cider vinegar, chili sauce, Worcestershire sauce, and tomato paste. Mix in the onion, brown sugar, and cayenne pepper.
  • Step 3 Heat oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, and saute the chicken thighs until browned. Remove from heat, drain, and arrange in a medium baking dish. Cover with the cider vinegar sauce mixture.
  • Step 4 Bake covered 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken is no longer pink and juices run clear.
